- **Capital markets – CSRC signals stronger stabilisation tools:** The CSRC’s post-close regulatory-work statement called for more precise counter-cyclical adjustment, a steady increase in the scale and proportion of long-term funds entering equities, and stronger preparations against global volatility and cross-border risk transmission. The message supports brokers, insurers and higher-quality listed companies after recent market swings. - **PBOC – RMB500bn MLF operation today:** The central bank said it will conduct RMB500bn of one-year medium-term lending facility operations on 24 July through fixed-quantity, rate-tender and multiple-price allocation. The injection is intended to keep banking-system liquidity ample and is the main onshore funding event before the open. [[Eastmoney](https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607233819292392.html)]
