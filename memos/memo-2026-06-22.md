**China policy & regulation**

* **LPR held a 13th month**: The PBOC fixed the 1-year LPR at 3.00% and the 5-year-plus (mortgage benchmark) at 3.50%, unchanged since Jun 2025 — no fresh easing impulse for banks or property, and a mild letdown for cut-positioned longs. [[中新网](https://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2026/06-22/10644761.shtml)]
* **State Council unveils 15-point foreign-investment plan (today)**: At a 22 Jun SCIO briefing, MOFCOM Vice-Minister Ling Ji detailed the "Stabilise & Upgrade Foreign Investment Action Plan" (MOFCOM/NDRC/MoF + 27 agencies). Highlights: wider services opening (expanded biotech, value-added telecom and wholly-foreign-owned hospital pilots) and — notably for the desk — support for more foreign institutions to use risk-management tools *including government-bond futures*, plus foreign fund-advisory. A concrete onshore market-access widening, reform-positive for FDI-sensitive names. [[中新网](https://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2026/06-22/10645035.shtml)] [[新华网](https://www.news.cn/fortune/20260622/10eaa7431498452aaec5c4e1dc481c7e/c.html)]
* **Lujiazui Forum microstructure (17–18 Jun, repriced on reopen)**: PBOC Gov. Pan Gongsheng narrowed the rate corridor to a symmetric ±50bp (from 70bp), cutting the overnight reverse-repo rate 25bp, and launched an offshore central-bank repo tool letting foreign central banks/SWFs tap RMB liquidity against high-grade bonds (CGBs) — RMB-internationalisation positives for HKEX and the big banks. [[新华网](https://www.news.cn/20260617/f4f7437109be4cb2a8f871f283cb0d09/c.html)] [[证券时报](https://www.stcn.com/topic/detail/587.html)]
* **CSRC widens STAR, warns on AI froth**: Chairman Wu Qing extended the STAR Market's fifth (pre-profit) listing standard to AI and hard-tech (quantum, biomanufacturing, embodied intelligence) while pledging to crack down on AI-theme speculation — structurally bullish hard-tech, but a cap on concept-stock froth. [[新华网](https://www.news.cn/20260617/f4f7437109be4cb2a8f871f283cb0d09/c.html)]
* **SAMR draws a line under the delivery subsidy war**: SAMR's draft guidelines (17 Jun) ban long-term, large-scale and below-cost subsidies; Meituan (3690 HK), Alibaba/Taobao Flash (9988 HK) and JD (9618 HK) all pledged support. An "anti-involution" margin-positive for the platforms after a cash-burning price war. [[中新网](https://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2026/06-17/10642493.shtml)] [[证券时报](https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3967207.html)]
* **NDRC front-loads consumer trade-ins**: A third batch of ~RMB62.5bn in consumption trade-in subsidies is to be released before end-June — supportive for appliance, auto and handset names. [[中新网](https://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2026/06-18/10642872.shtml)]
