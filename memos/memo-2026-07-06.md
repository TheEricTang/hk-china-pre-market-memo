- A-share trading rules go live today: the revised SSE/SZSE/BSE trading rules take effect this session. After-hours fixed-price trading (15:05–15:30) is extended from STAR/ChiNext to all A-shares and ETFs; main-board ST/*ST daily price limits widen from 5% to 10% (150+ stocks affected); ChiNext introduces market makers and moves block-trade confirmation to intraday; fund closing prices shift to a closing auction. First-day mechanics may change close and after-hours liquidity patterns today. [[STCN](https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998631.html)] [[STCN](https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998730.html)]

- PBOC liquidity injection today: the central bank announced Friday evening it will conduct a RMB1,000bn 3-month (91-day) outright reverse repo operation on 6 July — per Pengpai, the first month of net injection via the tool after four consecutive months of net withdrawal. A supportive funding signal for banks, brokers and rate-sensitive A/H names into the new week. [[Eastmoney](https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607033793725844.html)]

- CSRC refinancing overhaul: the CSRC opened consultation Friday on revised refinancing rules — a shelf-registration system for competitive private placements (one registration, multiple issues, 2-year validity), a unified market-price pricing mechanism, and simplified conditions for placements to controlling shareholders. The small-fast refinancing cap doubles from RMB300m to RMB600m (RMB1bn for issuers with net assets above RMB100bn; BSE cap RMB100m→200m). Positive for brokers' investment-banking pipelines and frequent A-share issuers. [[STCN](https://www.stcn.com/article/detail/3998730.html)] [[Eastmoney](https://finance.eastmoney.com/a/202607053794319895.html)]

Samsung has verbally notified customers of a ~20% QoQ rise in Q3 DRAM average selling prices, confirmed by two industry sources; TrendForce sees Q3 conventional DRAM contract prices +13–18% QoQ and NAND +10–15%, while UBS raised its Q3 DDR forecast to +32% QoQ (from +17%) and NAND to +30%. Fresh catalyst for the HK/A storage, module and semi-equipment complex at the open. A cost consideration for commercial-vehicle and hybrid makers rather than EV passenger-car names.
