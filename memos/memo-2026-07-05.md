- PBOC restarts net injection – RMB1tn outright reverse repo Monday: The PBOC announced Friday evening it will conduct RMB1,000bn of 3-month (91-day) outright reverse repo on Monday 6 Jul against RMB800bn maturing this month — a net RMB200bn add that ends several months of shrinking rollovers. July has RMB1.7tn of outright reverse repos plus RMB400bn of MLF maturing; DR001/DR007 have risen back to the ~1.4% policy rate, and analysts (CITIC's Ming Ming, Golden Credit's Wang Qing) read the move as a return to supportive net injection of medium-term liquidity.
