  1. 01

    Overnight US markets: All three indices closed higher Monday, with the Dow up 0.29% at 53,055.91 — its first close above 53,000 and a fresh record — the S&P 500 up 0.72% at 7,537.43 and the Nasdaq up 1.12% at 26,121.16, led by chips. Cross-assets: COMEX gold +1.23% to $4,176.3/oz and silver +2.33% to $62.485 (spot gold eased 0.25% to $4,165.13), WTI settled -0.13% at $68.60; US 10y yield fell 1.4bp to 4.4693% and 2y 2.7bp to 4.1101%, DXY flat at 100.853; LME copper +$37 to $13,404/t and tin +$436 to $53,064/t; onshore CNY closed 6.7925, down 121 pips.     Fed signals this week: The June FOMC meeting minutes are due this week, a focal point for the HK rate/liquidity backdrop. Governor Christopher Waller said forward guidance on the rate path "can still play an important role" if used properly — a measured pushback after new Chair Kevin Warsh pledged to de-emphasise forward guidance in favour of data-driven communication. [Eastmoney]

  3. 03

    Samsung Q2 prelim due today — storage complex catalyst: Samsung Electronics releases preliminary Q2 results Tuesday; LSEG consensus is revenue ~KRW170tn (~US$111bn) and operating profit ~KRW86tn (~US$56bn), up roughly 18x y/y and a third straight record, driven by AI memory demand. Samsung is up ~159% YTD and whipsawed Monday as KOSPI fell over 2%; the HK-listed 2x long SK hynix ETP (7709 HK) fell hard intraday Monday. The print lands during today's HK/A session — direct read-through for the storage/memory chain (A-share storage names, HK semis). Meanwhile the memory squeeze is now hitting Chinese consumers: retail 1TB SSDs have roughly doubled from ~RMB500 to ~RMB1,000, with the AI-driven shortage expected to run into 2027. [Eastmoney] [Eastmoney 早餐]

  4. 04

    SK hynix US listing advances: SK hynix filed an amended prospectus with the SEC Monday US time, confirming 17.79m common shares (177.9m ADS, 10 ADS per share) to list on Nasdaq under "SKHY"; net proceeds are earmarked for its KRW45.5tn construction capex, including EUV lithography purchases. The filing gave US semicap equipment names fresh ammunition overnight; watch the Asia memory/semicap chain and hynix-linked HK ETPs today. [Eastmoney]

  5. 05

    Nvidia pushes back on supply-chain wobble — PCB names in focus: After research house SemiAnalysis said Monday that Nvidia's Kyber NVL144 rack architecture had hit a setback in PCB manufacturing — knocking Ibiden down as much as 10%, Kingboard Laminates (1888 HK) as much as 18% and Samsung Electro-Mechanics 11% — Nvidia responded that its roadmap is unchanged. Separately, Delta and ABB, core partners on Nvidia's 800V HVDC data-centre power scheme, said the plan is progressing normally despite "delay" chatter. Rebound watch on the HK/A PCB-CCL and power-equipment chains at the open. [Eastmoney 早餐]

  6. 06

    Broadcom–Apple custom silicon extended: Broadcom announced Monday it has extended its agreement with Apple to develop and supply custom chips (ASICs) through 2031. Read-through for the Apple/ASIC supply chain and the broader custom-compute theme in HK/A AI hardware names. [Eastmoney]

  7. 07

    Oil — Aramco cuts Asia OSP by most this century: Saudi Aramco cut its August Arab Light official selling price to Asia by $11/bbl to a $1.50 discount versus the regional benchmark — the first discount since the 2020 price war and the largest monthly cut since at least 2000 — as Gulf producers push more barrels through Hormuz following the US–Iran interim deal. Brent has now given back all of its conflict-period gains and physical discounts are at post-COVID extremes; buyers say Saudi barrels remain pricier than spot alternatives, implying further cuts if supply stays high. Lower feedstock costs favour Chinese refiners and airlines; watch CNOOC/PetroChina H-shares on the crude side. WTI settled at $68.60. [Sina]

Hengli Petrochemical said refining/PTA/polyester spreads recovered markedly. Confirms the petchem spread-repair theme flagged by Dongfang Shenghong's +987–1,195% guide on Sunday — sector-wide positive for A/H refining-chemical names. [STCN]

Cathode maker Ronbay Technology (688005 CH) guided a swing to H1 profit of RMB100–120m, with Q2 up an implied 662–834% q/q. Also in the batch: CVTE (002841 CH) +264.62–314.91%, Baodi Mining (601121 CH) +189–210.11% on strong gold prices, and Zhejiang Orient (600120 CH) +~114%. [STCN] [Eastmoney 早餐]

Supportive backdrop for the HK/A compute, optics and grid-equipment complex. [Eastmoney 早餐]
