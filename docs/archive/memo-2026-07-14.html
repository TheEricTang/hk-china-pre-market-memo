  5. 05

    Li Qiang economy symposium: Premier Li Qiang chaired a symposium with economists and entrepreneurs on Monday afternoon, calling for stronger countercyclical adjustment, full use of existing policies while preparing incremental ones, and systematic measures to release domestic demand — with service-sector expansion and new consumption growth points singled out. Supportive tone for consumer, services and property names into H2 policy season. [Sina/SSE]

  6. 06

    Consumption "15th Five-Year" plan approved: the State Council approved the Plan for Expanding Consumption, targeting total retail sales of around RMB60tn by 2030, a clearly higher household consumption rate, minimum-wage and income-growth mechanisms, "healthy development" of property, and consolidating the capital market's steady-improving trend to lift household property income. The plan also calls for a national integrated computing-power network and 5G/gigabit infrastructure. Broad positive for retail, consumer services, telcos and brokers. [Eastmoney]

Direct read-through for the HK innovative-drug and medtech complex. [Eastmoney]
