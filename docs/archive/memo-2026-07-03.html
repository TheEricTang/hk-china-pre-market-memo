  1. 01

    Overnight US – storage/semis extend losses on Meta compute resale, Dow at record: In the July 2 session the Dow closed up over 1% at a fresh record while storage and semis fell for a second day on Meta's plan to sell excess AI compute and model access as a cloud business — SanDisk dropped over 11%, Seagate and Western Digital over 7%, Micron nearly 5%, ARM/Qualcomm over 3%, with the Philadelphia Semiconductor Index down 4%. Direct read-through at the open for the HK/A storage and AI-hardware chain (SMIC 981 HK, Hua Hong 1347 HK, NAURA 002371 CH), which sold off hard Thursday. [Eastmoney] [Sina]

  2. 02

    US June payrolls miss (released 20:30 HKT), Fed hike bets pushed back: Nonfarm payrolls rose just 57k vs ~115k expected, with April–May revised down a combined 74k; unemployment fell to 4.2% but participation slipped to 61.5% and leisure/hospitality shed 61k jobs. Odds of a July Fed hike — priced near 30% pre-data — were slashed and tightening expectations pushed toward December; the dollar index fell to around 100.8 and 2y/10y Treasury yields dropped 4–5bp. [Sina/FX678] [Eastmoney]

    Gold back above $4,100 overnight: Spot gold jumped over 2% to about $4,125/oz and silver rose over 3% to about $61.1/oz after the payrolls miss pared hike expectations and weakened the dollar. Supportive at the open for gold miners (Zijin 2899 HK, Chifeng 600988 CH and peers) after Thursday's rally in the sector. [Sina] [Sina/FX678]

    Unitree IPO registration approved (announced Thursday evening): The CSRC cleared Unitree Robotics' STAR Market IPO registration on July 2 evening, setting up A-shares' first embodied-intelligence/humanoid-robot listing. The company plans to raise RMB4.2bn (≥40.45m shares, ≥10% of enlarged capital); revenue grew from RMB159m in 2023 to RMB1.70bn in 2025 (CAGR 227%) with recurring net profit swinging to RMB591m. Fresh sentiment catalyst for the HK/A robotics complex at the open. [STCN]

Parent CSPC Pharma 1093 HK already rose 8.3% Thursday; the A-share reacts today, and the deal adds to the China innovative-drug licensing theme. [Eastmoney]
